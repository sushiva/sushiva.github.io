VISUAL DESIGN ELEMENTS:

✓ Color Scheme:
  - Primary: Cyan (#00d9ff)
  - Accent: Teal (#4ecdc4)
  - Background: Dark Navy (#0f0f1e)
  - Text: White/Gray

✓ Animations:
  - Smooth scroll between sections
  - Fade-in on scroll (projects, timeline)
  - Progress bars animate when visible
  - Hover effects on cards and buttons

✓ Responsive Breakpoints:
  - Desktop: 1920px, 1440px, 1024px
  - Tablet: 768px
  - Mobile: 576px, 375px

✓ Typography:
  - Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
  - Headings: 600 weight
  - Body: 400 weight

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

INTERACTION PATTERNS:

🖱️ Navigation:
   - Fixed navbar (always visible)
   - Active link highlights current section
   - Smooth scroll on click
   - Mobile: Hamburger menu

🖱️ Projects:
   - Hover: Image zooms, overlay appears
   - Click image overlay: Open demo/GitHub
   - Responsive grid: 3 cols → 2 cols → 1 col

🖱️ Skills:
   - Progress bars animate on scroll
   - Hover: Slight scale effect on category cards

🖱️ Contact:
   - Form validation on submit
   - Success/error notifications
   - Email/link hover effects
